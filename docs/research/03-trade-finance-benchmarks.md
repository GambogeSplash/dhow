## Details + sources

1. **Trade finance gap $2.5T** — ADB *Trade Finance Gaps, Growth, and Jobs Survey* (9th ed.), 2023–24 data, ~10% of global merchandise trade. https://www.adb.org/publications/adb-global-trade-finance-gap-survey | https://www.gtreview.com/news/global/trade-finance-gap-stabilises-at-us2-5tn/

2. **SME rejection 41%** (ADB, down from 45%) — and notably near the 40% large-corporate rate now, so don't over-claim "SMEs uniquely shut out" to a sharp banker. WTO frames it starker: ~half of SME applications rejected vs 7% for multinationals. Cleanest line: **"SME trade-finance applications rejected ~41% of the time (ADB 2024) vs ~7% for multinationals (WTO)."** https://www.gtreview.com/news/global/half-of-sme-trade-finance-requests-rejected-wto-finds/

3. **LC**: 7–10d issuance, 2–4wk full cycle; buyer fees 0.75–1.5% (some 0.75–2%+); amendment $50–300, discrepancy $50–200. Industry-typical ranges, not a single regulator. https://gocardless.com/guides/posts/how-much-does-letter-credit-cost/

4. **Correspondent banking**: 3–5 business days; ~¼ of corridors >3%; UAE→India efficient (~1.5%, World Bank); global remittance avg 6.49% (Q1 2025). Modernization: UPI–UAE linkage, Project mBridge (BIS + China/HK/Thailand/UAE multi-CBDC). https://remittanceprices.worldbank.org/ | https://www.aciworldwide.com/cross-border-payments-landscape-challenges-and-innovations

5. **UAE remittance ~$38.5B (2023, WB outflows)** — UAE doesn't fully report, likely understated; **do not cite $50B**. Crypto "30%" is a misread of **33% YoY on-chain value growth** ($56B+ received Jul24–Jun25, Chainalysis), not population ownership. **Stablecoins ~51%** of UAE crypto activity (confirmed). https://www.chainalysis.com/blog/middle-east-north-africa-crypto-adoption-2025/

6. **Jebel Ali 15.5M TEU (2024)**, capacity 19.4M, ~18% of DP World's 88.3M global. https://www.dpworld.com/en/news/dp-world-records-highest-cargo-volumes-at-jebel-ali-port-since-2015

7. **E-commerce $8.8B (2024, EZDubai×Euromonitor primary)** → $13.8B by 2029; 3rd-party estimates $11–17B. **Dubai** Cashless Strategy = 90% cashless by 2026 (launched Oct 2024, Dubai Finance), not UAE-wide. https://www.wam.ae/en/article/bjsbc4q-uae-e-commerce-market-reaches-aed323-billion-2024 | https://dubaicashless.ae/

## Three claims most likely to be challenged in the room — fix before pitching
- The **$50B remittance** claim → use **$38.5B**.
- **"30% crypto adoption"** → reframe as **33% YoY on-chain value growth**.
- **90% cashless** → it's **Dubai**, not UAE-wide.
- Bonus: update gap **$2T → $2.5T** (helps the case).
